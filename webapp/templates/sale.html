Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do I need any technical skills? ▸
SafeNet is built for non-technical parents. Download, double-click, and your browser opens to the dashboard. That's it.
Is SafeNet really free? ▸
Yes. All features are free. We accept optional donations to cover hosting and development costs, but you'll never hit a paywall.
How does reporting work? ▸
You fill out a simple form with the platform and profile URL. SafeNet generates a report you can submit to NCMEC's CyberTipline. All data stays on your computer unless you choose to send it.
Can my children use it too? ▸
Absolutely. The lessons and scenarios are age-appropriate and designed for families to go through together. The dashboard is parent-focused.
Is my data private? ▸
Yes. SafeNet runs locally on your computer. Reports are stored locally until you choose to submit them externally. We don't sell or share your data.
Does it work on Mac? ▸
The web app works on any OS with Python. The Windows EXE is a convenience wrapper — the full app can run on Mac or Linux via the command line.
