External Resources

Trusted organizations working to protect children online.

🏛️

National Center for Missing & Exploited Children

NCMEC leads the fight against child sexual exploitation. Operates the CyberTipline at report.cybertrip.org.

🔵

FBI Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3)

File complaints about internet-related crimes including online exploitation, fraud, and cyber abuse.

🌱

Thorn

Builds technology to defend children from sexual abuse. Their tools help law enforcement identify victims faster.

📱

Common Sense Media

Reviews apps, games, movies, and media for age-appropriateness. Provides detailed privacy evaluations.

🌐

Internet Matters

Age-specific guides and practical advice for keeping children safe online, organized by age group.

🛡️

FBI Online Safety Resources

FBI guides for parents, educators, and kids covering online safety, scams, and reporting.

📞

Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline

24/7 professional crisis counselors. Call or text 1-800-422-4453. Services in 170+ languages.

💬

Crisis Text Line

Free, 24/7 support via text message. Text HOME to 741741 from anywhere in the US.
